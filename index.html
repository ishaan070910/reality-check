About Reality Check

Reality Check is a decision-simulation tool that helps you visualise the probable outcomes of your choices across three time horizons: short-term, mid-term, and long-term.

The simulator is designed to plug into any AI backend (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) via the simulateDecision() function. Currently running with a smart placeholder engine.

Best used as a journalling prompt, a pre-decision sanity check, or a thinking tool—not a crystal ball.
